Introducing Pico


A stupidly simple & blazing fast,flat file CMS.

Making the web easy.





Stupidly Simple


Picos makes creating and maintaining a website as simple as editing text lines.

Blazing Fast


Picos is seriously lightweight and doesn't use a database.

No Database


Picos is a "flat file" CMS ,meaning no database and no MySql queries,nothing.


Markdown Formatting


Editing your website in your favourite editor.

Twing Template


Picos use he twing templating engine.For powerful and flexible theme.


Open Source


Pico is completely free and open source.
